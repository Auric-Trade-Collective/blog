If you scanned our flyer or want us to tell you why to join Auric Trade Collective, this is for you! Have you ever used a platform that you really liked? Something you use everyday. Excruciatingly, endlessly, joyfully. What memories do you have of such a platform? For many people they will say such a platform was once great, but fell away. Such platforms have failed to listen to their users, their people, their very backing for existence.

We feel your pain. We *were* you at one point in our lives. All of us. Everybody backing our mission has been betrayed by what they once thought was great. And for that, we formed Auric Trade Collective. Our mission is to provide software and a movement that listen to its user base. Our products are governed by those who build them, not shareholders. And our community is given influence over our products as well. Auric Trade Collective is proud to be a community oriented worker cooperative.

Our movement is very small right now, and that's why we're looking for like-minded people. As of now we are composed of 7 people. Here are the early people behind our movement:

We have 4 more people who would be eager to see newcomers to our movement! They're actively working to make our projects move along.

### Our Projects
We've been working on a number of projects to get our movement off the ground, these vary from games to webservers.

 **Nox, Our Body**: Nox is a webserver that Yendy has been working hard on for the collective. It's written in golang and is tailored to our needs and vision as a collective. You can check it out [here](https://github.com/Auric-Trade-Collective/nox).
 
**The Grid, Our Connections**: The Grid is a direct outcome of what Auric Trade Collective stands for. It's a project idea which sprouted from what we see as the rubble of discord. We believe that we can do better. This project is written with Golang, Typescript, and Svelte.

**The Space, Our World**: The Space is another outcome of our mission. From the dying community of Rec Room sprouted an idea to build a community oriented game. Written in C# in Unity, The Space represents our ambition to create a community oriented world. Something really for the people.

**Nexus, Our Universe**: Nexus, as an idea, is more of a network. It's the representation of the connection between these three projects. And it's our underlying mission in the flesh.
