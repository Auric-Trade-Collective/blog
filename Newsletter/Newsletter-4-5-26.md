While we don't have a big story to share with you as we did in [[Newsletter-3-31-26|our first newsletter]], we are happy to say that we have begun work on our RecRoom inspired game, Nexus. Our game design documents are accessible through our [Discord server](https://discord.gg/tJ4qVR3xGM), or by emailing one of us:

- Set a deadline for these projects
