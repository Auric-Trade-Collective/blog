Slow days are not fun! But we are experiencing some right now. We are so very thankful for our influx of community members. But we're bogged down due to lack of developers recently. Don't fear, we aren't shutting down, development is just slow. That being said we did accomplish some big things this week:

- Yendy and Trollage this week reconfigured our servers to be more reliable. Previously we were using podman (Yendy wishes we still were) which was giving us difficulties, however, we are now using Docker which is more of an industry standard.
- Yendy also pushed out version 0.0.3 of [Nox](https://github.com/Auric-Trade-Collective/nox).
- Tiago launched development on The Space.
- We passed some internal bylaws on our organization, which you can read more about [[Voting In Auric|here]].
- We launched development on our official website.

With our accomplishments this week, we also met some difficulties. It's rather hard for us to find consistent developers. We actually ask anyone interested in developing for us to contact us.
